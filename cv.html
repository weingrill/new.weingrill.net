Motivation

After my parents got me a book about stars, planets and other worlds at the age of eight, I immeditly fell in love with astronomy. At the age of 26 I bought my first telescope to observe a comet.

Back in 2002 I decided to drop my career in IT business and started to go back to University once again to study physics and astrophysics.

Nowadays I am retrofitting the BMK camera within the BMK10k project that will observe active stars for the PLATO2.0 mission.
